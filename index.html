About Us

We have been working with property owners and operators to maintain and improve the performance of building facades and roofs for over a decade, undertaking projects ranging from major façade refurbishment programmes to planned preventative maintenance and individual component replacement & repair.

We provide a wide range of commercial façade services to a diverse client base including leading property management companies, national construction companies, and local government.

Our Services

Commercial Glazing

Overhauling and improving window operation as a cost-effective alternative to replacement, including:

  • Glass replacement
  • Seal and gasket renewal
  • Window furniture upgrading
  • Opening winder mechanism servicing
  • Removal of scratches and dents from powder coated finishes

Façade and Cladding

Full survey / design / fabrication / install service for PPC aluminium rainscreen and roofing components.

  • Leak investigation, including electrostatic membrane testing
  • Prepolymer liquid applied repair to commercial roofing systems
  • Application of safety film & bespoke branding design to entrance screens

Safety

Risk Assessments and detailed Method Statements are all site-specific and non-generic.

Key management and supervisory staff hold SMSTS qualifications and all operatives have passed the CITB Health & Safety Test and hold a CSCS card as a minimum, plus IPAF, PASMA, and IRATA qualifications depending on operational requirements.

Our Health & Safety procedures are externally accredited and are constantly being reviewed and upgraded.
